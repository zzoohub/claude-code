# Email Types Reference

Complete catalog of email types by category for SaaS lifecycle marketing.

---

## Onboarding Emails

| Email | Trigger | Goal |
|-------|---------|------|
| Welcome email | Signup | Deliver value, set expectations |
| Getting started | Day 1 | First action guidance |
| Quick win tip | Day 2-3 | Drive to first value |
| Feature discovery | Day 3-7 | Expand usage |
| Incomplete setup reminder | 24h, 72h after incomplete setup | Re-engage |
| Activation celebration | User reaches Aha Moment | Reinforce + next step |
| Team invite prompt | After activation | Expand usage |

---

## Retention Emails

| Email | Trigger | Goal |
|-------|---------|------|
| Upgrade to paid | After trial/activation | Convert free → paid |
| Upgrade to higher plan | Approaching limits | Expand revenue |
| Ask for review | After positive experience | Social proof |
| Proactive support | Support ticket pattern | Prevent churn |
| Product usage report | Weekly/monthly | Show value delivered |
| NPS survey | 30/60/90 day marks | Measure satisfaction |
| Referral program | After positive NPS | Viral growth |
| Feature announcement | New feature launch | Re-engage + expand |

---

## Billing Emails

| Email | Trigger | Goal |
|-------|---------|------|
| Switch to annual | After 3+ months on monthly | Increase LTV |
| Failed payment (dunning) | Payment failure | Recover revenue |
| Card expiring | 30/15/7 days before expiry | Prevent failure |
| Cancellation survey | Cancel initiated | Feedback + save |
| Renewal reminder | 30 days before annual renewal | Set expectations |
| Receipt/invoice | After payment | Transaction record |

---

## Usage Emails

| Email | Trigger | Goal |
|-------|---------|------|
| Daily/weekly summary | Scheduled | Show value, drive return |
| Key event notification | Specific event | Real-time value |
| Milestone celebration | User reaches milestone | Positive reinforcement |
| Inactivity alert | X days inactive | Re-engage |

---

## Win-Back Emails

| Email | Trigger | Goal |
|-------|---------|------|
| Trial expired | Trial end + no conversion | Convert |
| Cancelled customer | After cancellation | Reactivate |
| "We miss you" | 30 days post-cancel | Re-engage |
| What's new | 60 days post-cancel | Show improvements |
| Special offer | 90 days post-cancel | Final re-engagement |

---

## Campaign Emails

| Email | Frequency | Goal |
|-------|-----------|------|
| Monthly newsletter | Monthly | Engagement, traffic |
| Seasonal promotion | Quarterly | Revenue boost |
| Product update | As needed | Feature adoption |
| Industry news roundup | Weekly/monthly | Thought leadership |
| Pricing update | As needed | Transparency |
| Case study | Monthly | Social proof, conversion |
